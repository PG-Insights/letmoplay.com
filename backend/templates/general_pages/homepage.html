Who are we?

At Let MO Play, we are dedicated to raising awareness about the benefits of legalizing sports betting in Missouri. We are committed to promoting local businesses and highlighting the positive impacts that legal sports betting can have on the state's economy.

As lifelong residents of Missouri, we are deeply connected to our state and committed to promoting policies that benefit our communities. We believe that legalizing sports betting can provide a new means of revenue for the state and create new job opportunities for residents.

  • We use social media to connect with residents and politicians in Missouri to create a dialogue about the benefits for local businesses and the state's economy.
  • Let MO Play collaborates with local businesses that can benefit from legal sports betting, such as sports bars and restaurants.
  • We prioritize responsible betting. Resources such as National Council on Problem Gambling and Gambler's Anonymous are available if needed.

Let MO Play is committed to promoting responsible gambling and increasing sports engagement to provide a boost to local economies. Plus, legalization can help combat illegal sports betting and match-fixing, ensuring the integrity of sports competitions. Don't miss out on the chance to be a part of this exciting movement!

Missourians are losing millions in tax dollars

According to the American Gaming Association, sports betting generated a record $4.33 Billion in revenue. It remains to be seen what, exactly, the tax rate would be if Missouri were to legalize sports betting. However, published estimates consistently predict legalizing sports betting would generate $20-40 million in additional tax revenue for the state's education system.

  • 01 What have other states done?

    Colorado generated almost $44,000,000 in additional tax revenue in the first 3 years since legalizing sports betting in May of 2020.

  • 02 Who benefits from legalization?

    The answer is small businesses and residents. Local bars and restaurants will have access to an entirely new marketing stream, which could attract new customers.

  • 03 How much can we gain?

    As a demonstration of what sports betting can do for the local economy - New Jersey sportsbooks generated $82.2 million in revenue in the month of May, 2023. Ending prohibition will create jobs in a variety of sectors, including casinos, sportsbooks, and technology companies with the potential to create thousands of jobs in the state of Missouri.
