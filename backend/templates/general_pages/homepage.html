Who are we?

At Let MO Play, we are dedicated to raising awareness about the benefits of legalizing sports betting in Missouri. Our business is committed to promoting local businesses and highlighting the positive impacts that legal sports betting can have on the state's economy.

As lifelong residents of Missouri, we are deeply connected to our state and committed to promoting policies that benefit our communities. We believe that legalizing sports betting can provide a new means of revenue for the state and create new job opportunities for residents.

  • We use social media to connect with residents and politicians in Missouri to create a dialogue about the benefits for local businesses and the state's economy.
  • Let MO Play collaborates with local businesses that can benefit from legal sports betting, such as sports bars and restaurants.
  • We prioritize responsible betting. Resources such as National Council on Problem Gambling and Gambler's Anonymous are available if needed.

Let MO Play is committed to promoting responsible gambling and increasing sports engagement to provide a boost to local economies. Plus, legalization can help combat illegal sports betting and match-fixing, ensuring the integrity of sports competitions. Don't miss out on the chance to be a part of this exciting movement!

Missourians are losing millions in tax dollars

According to the American Gaming Association, the total revenue generated by legal sports betting in the United States in 2020 was $1.5 billion. Of this amount, state and local governments collected approximately $245 million in tax revenue.

  • 01 What have other states done?

    In 2020, the state of Colorado collected $3.4 million in tax revenue from sports betting in just its first month of operation and Missouri has already broken one Colorado record after legalization.

  • 02 Who benefits from legalization?

    The answer is small businesses and residents. Local bars and restaurants that offer sports betting services can attract customers who may not have otherwise visited their establishments.

  • 03 How much can we gain?

    Atlantic City saw a 20% increase in revenue from hotel room rentals and a 14% increase in revenue from food and beverage sales. Ending prohibition will create jobs in a variety of sectors, including casinos, sportsbooks, and technology companies that provide software and services to the industry with the potential to create more than 200,000 jobs in the United States.
